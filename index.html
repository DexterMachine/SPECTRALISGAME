GAME INFO

Spectralis is a galaxy in which you can farm $ZENIA, make friends, create clans, and face your enemies. You will have missions, and great rewards for completing them. Do you have the necessary guts for this adventure?

LAND IN SPECTRALIS

This is a plot of land in Spectralis, where players will be able to farm and increase their $ZENIA production, as will provide bonuses to various statistics, both in clan wars and missions against bosses on different planets. Initially, players will start with a small plot. To increase their production on a large scale, we will be selling plots during the pre-sale stage.

CLAN AND WARS

Choose your clan friends well, as they will be the ones who will fight alongside you in wars against other clans and bosses.

CHARACTER CREATIONS

In this section you can create your character wich will be used in the game. You will also be able to import the nft spaceships you have obtained. Soon it will be implemented that players can also import their nft character.

CHAT AND REWARDS

You can talk to your friends, see the rewards you will receive in $Zenia for completing missions and more.

MARKETPLACE

In the marketplace, players will be able yo buy, sell and trade their in-game NFT, setting prices they deem fair. Theyll also have access to the staking section to lock their coins and receive wonderful rewards in $ZENIA. This way, a farming cycle will be generated where everyone wins.
